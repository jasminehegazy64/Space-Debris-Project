About Us

ESPACIO : Detection, Tracking, Collision Prediction of Space Debris

Welcome to Espacio, your go-to platform for space debris detection, tracking, and collision reduction. In a time where space debris poses a growing threat, Espacio is dedicated to ensuring the safety of space missions. We use advanced technology to identify and track debris, providing real-time data for space agencies to navigate safely. Join us in raising awareness and finding sustainable solutions for a cleaner and safer space environment. Explore the cosmos responsibly with Espacio.
